02. Addresses & Geography

House name/number, street name, and town. Essential for finding the family on Census returns (e.g. 1921, 1939 Register, 1950 Census).
Where did you live when you first married, or voted? Helps with Electoral Roll searches.
Did the family move across borders? Note the approximate years and destination ports for Passenger List searches.

03. Parents & Siblings

Do you know roughly when and where they married? (Town, specific church/registry office).
List brothers and sisters, including those who may have passed away in childhood. Note birth years if known.

What are your earliest memories? How were holidays celebrated?
How did major historical events (e.g., WWII, rationing, local events) impact your immediate family?
